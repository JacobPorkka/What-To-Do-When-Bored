Why going to bed can help with you being bored

- Going to bed early is very good for you

- It can help to clear your mind and think about things in a different way

- Getting lots of sleep also helps you to have a better day the next day and be full of energy.

- I do this often when I am bored because I can sleep and wake up being more motivated
